
# FUTURE_PE_03

## 🧠 Task 03 – AI SEO Blog & Content Cluster Generator

### 📌 Task Overview
This project demonstrates the use of **Prompt Engineering** to generate SEO-optimized blog content and a content cluster strategy for a healthcare business.

- **Track:** Prompt Engineering (PE)  
- **Business:** VitaCare Multi-Specialty Clinic  
- **Location:** Hyderabad, Telangana  
- **Tools Used:** Claude AI, Google Docs, GitHub  

---

## 🎯 Objective
To create SEO-driven content using AI, including:
- Keyword research & intent mapping  
- SEO blog structure and content  
- Content cluster & internal linking strategy  
- Local SEO optimization  

---

## 🧠 SEO Content Strategy
A **pillar + cluster model** was used:
- Pillar Page: *General Physician in Hyderabad*  
- Supporting blogs target long-tail keywords  
- Internal linking improves **Google rankings & authority**

---

## 🔑 Keyword & Content Cluster

### Pillar Keyword
- **General Physician in Hyderabad** (Transactional)

### Cluster Keywords
- When to see a general physician  
- Diabetes management tips Hyderabad  
- Preventive health check-up cost Hyderabad  
- Signs you need cardiology consultation  
- Best time for annual health check-up  

---

## 📝 SEO Blog Content

### Blog Title
**When Should You See a General Physician in Hyderabad?**

### Key Sections
- What does a general physician treat?  
- Signs you should visit a doctor  
- Importance of early diagnosis  
- What to expect during consultation  
- How to choose the right doctor  

---

### Sample Insight
A general physician is the first point of contact for diagnosing and managing multiple health conditions, helping patients avoid unnecessary delays and complications.

---

## 🔗 Content Cluster Strategy

- 5 supporting blogs connected to pillar page  
- Each blog links to:
  - Pillar page  
  - Other related blogs  
  - Service/appointment pages  

👉 This creates a **hub-and-spoke SEO structure**

---

## 📍 Local SEO Optimization

Content adapted for:
- Banjara Hills  
- Gachibowli  
- Secunderabad  

👉 Improves visibility in location-based searches

---

## 📊 Key Learnings

- Keyword intent is crucial for SEO success  
- Structured prompts improve content quality  
- Internal linking boosts search rankings  
- Local SEO increases discoverability
